Frequently Asked Questions

What is a favicon?

A favicon is the small icon displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and history. It helps users identify your website at a glance. Standard sizes include 16x16, 32x32, 48x48, and 64x64 pixels.

How do I add a favicon to my website?

Add this to your HTML <head>: <link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="32x32" href="favicon-32x32.png">. For ICO format, use: <link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico">.
